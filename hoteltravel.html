Travel Accomodation

For information about hotel reservation, see below.

RCDDS 2018 will be hosted by SRM University, Vadapalani.

Bus

The total straight line distance between Chennai Airport and Vadapalani is 10.7 km (kilometers). The total travel time is approx 30 mins.
The total straight line distance between Chennai Central Railway Station and Vadapalani is 10.7 km (kilometers). The total travel time is approx 50 mins.

Taxi

Taxi takes 20 minutes from Chennai Airport to SRM University, Vadapalani.
Taxi takes 40 minutes from Chennai Airport to SRM University, Vadapalani.

Hotel Reservation

Accomodation will not be provided in college campus. Hotel bookings can be done here.

RCDDS 2018

RCDDS 2018 aims to bring together researchers from industry, academic researchers, Business Delegates, scholars and graduate students to exchange and share their experiences, new ideas, and research results across aspects of Big-Data and Decision Sciences.
